The Geospatial Computer Vision group's focus is to use satellite and drone imagery to provide insights in various applications. Our current projects are in:
1. **Agriculture**
Preparing systems for information aid to farmers and district level managers. We will be using multispectral drones as well as ground sensors for various regions in India.

2. **Urban Planning**
Systems to monitor land use, roofs, trees, lakes, etc. in cities across India (and eventually, the world) based on remote sensing data. We will be working on various aspects including heat island modeling, tree cover tracking and applications in urban planning.
