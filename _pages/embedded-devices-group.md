The embedded sensing and perception group focuses on creating embedded devices of the future. Be it on-field sensing for water quality, agriculture or a medtech device for continuous monitoring of blood sugar, these are the kind of devices we build in the group. There is synergy with the [Geospatial Computer Vision Group](/geospatial-computer-vision) where the sensing applications also enable a scaling path through remote sensing.
